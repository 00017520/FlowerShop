The History of Flowers

Timeline of Flowers

  • Ancient Civilizations:

    Cultures like Egypt, Mesopotamia, and China revered flowers for their beauty, symbolism, and spiritual importance.

  • Classical Era:

    Flowers gained mythological significance, celebrated in Greek and Roman rituals, art, and gardens.

  • Medieval Era:

    Flowers were central to religious symbolism, medicinal practices, and structured gardens in Europe.

  • Renaissance:

    Tulips became a luxury item, and flower breeding flourished for artistic and cultural expression.

  • Modern Era:

    Flowers became global symbols of remembrance, peace, and innovation, from poppies for WWI to lunar flower experiments.
